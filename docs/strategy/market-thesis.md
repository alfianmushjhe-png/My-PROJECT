# Market Thesis

A startup becomes important when it understands a market shift before the market becomes obvious. The company should document why the timing is right, why customers need a new solution, and why the opportunity can become large.

## Enterprise Notes

- Owner: Strategy Team
- Review posture: scale-minded
- Change class: startup planning foundation
- Sequence: 003/100

## Execution Standard

This document helps the startup convert ambition into operating clarity. It should be reviewed as the company learns from customers, hires talent, raises capital, and expands into a recognized market leader.
