# Wedge Strategy

A startup should enter the market through a focused wedge. The wedge creates early traction, proof, and customer love before expanding into a broader platform.

## Enterprise Notes

- Owner: Strategy Team
- Review posture: scale-minded
- Change class: startup planning foundation
- Sequence: 007/100

## Execution Standard

This document helps the startup convert ambition into operating clarity. It should be reviewed as the company learns from customers, hires talent, raises capital, and expands into a recognized market leader.
